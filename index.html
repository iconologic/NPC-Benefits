Our Story

NPC Benefits was initially founded in 1991 as National Planning Consultants, a specialty provider of supplemental disability plans for large employers. Today, NPC works jointly with group benefit consultants and other insurance professionals to bring the most innovative supplemental income protection concepts and products to large employers and associations, using customized enrollment communications and technology.

Our Purpose

To enhance and simplify the communication of income protection plans to employees, associations, individuals and families.

Having adequate income protection insurance is one of the most important aspects of financial planning, as it protects the single asset that provides for everything—your current and future income. New and innovative income protection plans are available today, but too many companies, associations and individuals aren’t aware or taking advantage of them.

Why?

The communication and enrollment of these plans has gotten overly complex, with jargon and numbers that often don’t translate clearly to the individual. Our communications are customized for each client, providing 1) optimal understanding of the value of income protection, and 2) multiple cost/benefit options to meet individual needs.

What We Do

Front-End Consultation

The custom exhibits we use in discussions with clients and decision-makers are both compelling and concise—providing the exact information they need to know and answering two questions: “Why is this important?” and “How will this benefit me and my organization?” Answering those two simple questions is essential to our ultimate goal—to make the best income protection plans available to as many individuals and families as we can.

Client-Carrier Optimization

With comprehensive knowledge of the insurance carrier landscape, we conduct thorough reviews of the most competitive offers in the marketplace—and then match clients with their best fit based on features, pricing and service.

Enrollment and Communication

To promote the highest levels of employee reception and participation, we customize our proprietary enrollment materials, strategies and technology—from logistics, all the way down to their look and feel—to match each client’s unique culture.

Administration

We stay engaged with our clients as the one-call contact to fulfill all ongoing service needs with the insurance carrier.
